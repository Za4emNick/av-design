Process

An interiors-only, turnkey workflow — from first conversation to styling.

01 · Discovery & Brief

Goals, lifestyle, budget, timeline. We align on scope and create a clear interior brief.

02 · Site Measure & Audit

On-site measurements, photos, and an inventory of existing pieces to keep or replace.

03 · Concept & Space Planning

Mood, palettes, and interior layouts (flow, adjacencies, furniture plans) that maximize light and usability.

04 · Materials, FF&E & Custom Millwork

Finishes, textiles, lighting, and bespoke cabinetry/details with elevations and schedules for trades.

05 · Specifications & Procurement

Final specs, quoting and ordering. Coordination with vendors and your GC for install readiness.

06 · Installation & Styling

Deliveries, white-glove installation, art & accessories, punch list — a complete, livable space.

*We specialize exclusively in interior design. Architectural and permitting services are not included. If necessary, we collaborate with your general contractor and/or architect, and such matters are clarified face-to-face.

About

AV Design Studio is a full-service interior design practice based in New York, dedicated to creating spaces that embody refined aesthetics, thoughtful functionality, and enduring quality.

We approach every project with a deep understanding of how people live and interact with their environment. Whether designing a new build, transforming an existing space, or curating a full-service furnishing plan, our process begins with listening — understanding your vision, lifestyle, and aspirations. Each project is meticulously crafted from concept to completion, guided by collaboration, precision, and an appreciation for timeless design.

Working closely with trusted artisans, builders, and vendors, we ensure that every detail contributes to a cohesive, elevated living experience that feels distinctly yours.
